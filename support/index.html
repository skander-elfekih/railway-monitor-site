Common Questions

How do I get my Railway API token?
Log in to railway.app, open your account settings, and navigate to the Tokens section. Create a new token and paste it into the Railway Monitor action settings in Stream Deck.
Where do I find my Service ID?
Open your project in the Railway dashboard, click on the service you want to monitor, and copy the service ID from the URL or the service settings panel.
The key isn't updating — what should I check?
Verify that your API token is valid and has not expired. Also ensure your service ID is correct. If the issue persists, try removing and re-adding the action in Stream Deck.
Which Stream Deck devices are supported?
Railway Monitor works with all Stream Deck models (Stream Deck, Stream Deck MK.2, Stream Deck XL, Stream Deck Mini, Stream Deck +, and Stream Deck Neo) running Stream Deck software version 6.4 or later.
Is Railway Monitor free?
Yes — Railway Monitor is completely free on the Elgato Stream Deck Marketplace. Download it and start monitoring your Railway.app services today.
